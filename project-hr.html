Modern Human Resources Management

Welcome to the Kartezya HR Management System. This platform is a next-generation, cloud-based Human Resources Management Software (HRMS) designed to help businesses optimize, automate, and organize their core human resources operations. By integrating employee tracking, department planning, leave flows, expense tracking, and event management into a unified SaaS dashboard, Kartezya HR streamlines workflows, minimizes paperwork, and enhances employee engagement.

By moving your human resource processes to Kartezya HR, you can simplify communication between management and teams, decrease processing times for claims and time-offs, and maintain secure digital records of your entire organizational structure.

System Modules & Capabilities

👥 Employee Directory & Profile Management

Easily add, edit, and archive employee records. Track personal information, employment files, contract details, performance metrics, job descriptions, and team assignments within a secure database compliant with data protection standards.

🏢 Department & Position Organization

Define hierarchical organizational structures, establish divisions, and assign team leads. Visualize reporting relationships and track employee capacity across departments dynamically.

📅 Leave & Vacation Management Flow

Simplify leave request submissions, manager approval workflows, and vacation calendars. Kartezya HR automatically updates individual time-off balances, maintains leave history, and generates detailed reports for payroll alignment.

💰 Expense & Claim Tracking

Accelerate the process of evaluating employee expense claims. Employees can upload invoices and submit expense requests, allowing managers to approve, reject, and track payment transactions directly through the dashboard.

🎉 Corporate Event Planning & Announcements

Schedule company-wide meetings, professional webinars, workshops, or team-building events. Publish internal announcements, manage RSVP lists, and coordinate employee participation seamlessly.
